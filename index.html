Manifesto


Gallerinsta is an innovative online art gallery. We use technology to develop
a 360-degree experience: free exhibitions, talks, chats and the unique possibility to buy your favorite masterpieces!

Gallerinsta is a space of dialogue, an agora where we give voice to female artists. The current art market in unfair, elitist, difficult to approach; we facilitate both artists and collectors to be aware of their role in the market.

Have you ever dreamt to become a collector, to join vernissages or simply to have a unique masterpiece in your bedroom? Gallerinsta is the way! A new inclusive way to collect, to be educated to art. We all are responsible for the future of artists and collectors; we need to play a role in the art market change and evolution.
